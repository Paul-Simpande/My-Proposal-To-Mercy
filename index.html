LOVE LETTER AND PROPOSAL

Hey Mama,

I’ve found myself thinking of you more often than I can count, and the feeling only seems to grow stronger each day. I've wanted to put these feelings into words for a while now, and though I know words can never fully capture the depth of how I feel, I hope this letter brings me closer to expressing what my heart has been aching to say.

I sensed something unique and inexplicable about your presence from the first time we met. Your warmth, humour, and kindness have a way of illuminating a space and attracting others to you. And once you smile, it seems as though time stands still for a moment, allowing me to appreciate the brightness you bring into my life.

It is found in the small things about you, such as the way you listen to others as if their opinions are important and the way you support and believe in them even when they don't believe in themselves.

You appear in every scene of the future I envision. I want to chat to you at the end of the day and celebrate all of my accomplishments, no matter how minor, with you. Just as you are, I want to be the one that supports and loves you without conditions. I would be incredibly proud to call you my own.

This may sound like a fantasy, but it's how I feel about every aspect of myself. I can't fathom my life without attempting to make it a reality after seeing what you've showed me. I would love nothing more than to discuss this with you if there's even the slightest possibility that you share my feelings.

So here I am, vulnerable and hopeful, asking if you’d consider giving us a chance. Whatever your answer, please know that you’ll always have someone who admires you deeply and wishes you all the happiness in the world.
